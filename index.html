Our Platform

Our system digitizes the process of mutual aid and ensures all of the utilities needs are quickly met. We minimize the labor required to get relevant information to responding utilities. Put simply, we make it efficient, fast, and easy to ensure the right work is being done at the correct location, in the best way possible. We take care of the communication, so that you don't have to.

Simple.

With features like auto form filling, automated email, and a simple interface, any action requires just a few clicks.

Smart.

All you manually enter is basic information. Our system supplies GPS information, summaries, asset histories, and mutual aid agreements.

Powerful.

Responding utilities know exactly where they're going, what work is needed, and have all of their needs met. All with a few simple clicks.
